Our Campus

Explore life at JC University Here at JC University, we want you to feel right at home. There's more or less everything you need on campus and for anything else, you have an entire city to choose from. You'll find us in the heart of JC's city centre where we're surrounded by supermarkets to suit all tastes, we have an abundance of high street stores along with loads of great little thrift stores, second-hand shops and boutiques, restaurants for every cuisine, clubs, theatres, museums - you name it, we have it. But you'll discover all that when you come to visit us! In the meantime, take a look at our campus.



The best thing about JC University is how world-class their research and facilities are. Your lecturers are all experts in their fields, but are also supportive and accessible if you need extra help or want to learn more.
